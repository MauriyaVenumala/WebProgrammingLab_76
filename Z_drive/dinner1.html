Grilled Lemon Garlic Salmon







 Ingredients

    
    
  • 2 salmon fillets
    • 
    
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 
    
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 
    
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
    • 
    
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 
    
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
    • 



 Directions

Step 1

Preheat grill to medium heat.

Step 2

Mix olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper. Marinate salmon for 15 minutes.

Step 3

Grill salmon for 4-5 minutes per side until flaky. Serve warm.
